Vision

I chose to pursue Industrial Design because I believe it brings together many areas of design and allows for a comprehensive, collaborative approach to problem-solving. Industrial Design offers the opportunity to work on diverse projects, enabling me to grow professionally across different fields while combining strategic thinking, aesthetic sensibility, and technology to create real and innovative solutions.

I see myself as a self-taught and curious person. Since I was a child, I've explored topics that caught my interest through hands-on learning. This has made me a persistent individual and helped me understand the value of process in achieving the best outcomes.

Let's work together!

If you are interested in my work or have any questions, feel free to contact me. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations.
